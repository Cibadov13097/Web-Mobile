Azerbaijan

The Land of Fire

Azerbaijan is a country located at the crossroads of Eastern Europe and Western Asia, bordered by the Caspian Sea to the east, Russia to the north, Georgia to the northwest, Armenia to the west, and Iran to the south.

Geography and Location 🗺

Capital:🗽 Baku, located on the Caspian Sea coast.
Area:🌍 Approximately 86,600 square kilometers.
Topography:🌋 Azerbaijan is geographically diverse, with a landscape that includes the Caspian Sea coastline, the flat plains of the Kura River, and the Greater and Lesser Caucasus mountain ranges.
Climate:⛅ The country experiences varied climates from subtropical in the lowlands to alpine in the mountains.

Culture of Azerbaijan 🗺

The culture of Azerbaijan (Azerbaijani: Azərbaycan mədəniyyəti) combines a diverse and heterogeneous set of elements which developed under the influence of Iranic, Turkic and Caucasian cultures. Azerbaijani culture includes its cuisine, literature, folk art, and music.
